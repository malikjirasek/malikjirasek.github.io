Scientific Interests

I believe that physics is one of many great approaches to understand how the complex interactions of nature create the world we live in. It provides the necessary tools to probe systems from microscopic up to macroscopic scales. I am especially interested in those systems where our intuition fails and we need these tools in order to systematically determine the behavior those systems.
