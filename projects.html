Master's thesis

Together with Viktoria Noel and Igor Lesanovsky, I am developing a field theoretical framework for computing the quantum Fisher information in driven-dissipative many-body quantum systems. The goal is to discriminate genuine quantum effects from classical behavior of these systems.

Bachelor's thesis

Together with Albert Cabot and Igor Lesanovsky, I studied the implementation of a so-called Boundary Time Crystal as a light source for measuring optical phases. Results of this work can be found in the attached paper and are currently under review at Physical Review Letters.

I spent 2 months in the Modern Optics group of Alejandro Saenz at Humboldt University of Berlin. Together with Matee ur Rehman I extended an existing simulation framework for ultracold atoms in optical lattices, allowing for the implementation of realistic inter-atomic interaction potentials.
