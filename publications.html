The Boundary Time Crystal as a light source for quantum enhanced sensing beyond the Heisenberg Limit

Jirasek, M., Lesanovsky, I., Cabot, A.

We propose the implementation of driven-dissipative many-body quantum systems as light sources in quantum sensing applications. We demonstrate their advantage by simulating the precision of sensing optical phases with a so-called Boundary Time Crystal as a light source.
